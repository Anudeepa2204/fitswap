The team and the story behind Fitswap

Ru Wikmann, the founder and CEO of Fitswap was born in Riga, Latvia but have lived in the UK for 12 years. Overcoming bone disease through fitness as a child inspired him to help others as a personal trainer. He then successfully pursued the challenge of becoming a WBFF Pro athlete, a highly respected accolade in the fitness world. Ru has already had a 10 year long career in the fitness industry in London, and he is now looking to create a bigger impact by leveraging technology to create this unique platform.

Viesturs Jegorovs, the Lead Developer and Designer was also born in Riga, Latvia. He has 15 years of full-stack development and graphic design experience, and he has been friends with Ru for just as long. There was a time when Viesturs asked Ru to write a personal training programme for his sister in exchange for making a website. This is how the idea of Fitswap was born.
